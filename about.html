About Us

Our company was founded in 2021, built around a group of veteran players who have a passion for the sport. Our headquarters is located in Los Angeles California where we constantly brainstorm new ideas on how to increase the intensity and overall fun of paintball. We work tirelessly to create new equipment to enhance player experience. Originally we started out as a group of friends who had this aspiration to make the absolute best paintball park in the world with limited restrictions and an abundance of freedom. We always dreamed of making our paintball park the most intense using the latest and greatest technology at hand to not only increase competition but to bring more realism and futuristic styles of warfare. Also working towards bringing ideas from top video game franchises such as, Call of Duty, Battlefield, Fortnite, and many more, to life.

-SPRAY N' PAINT
